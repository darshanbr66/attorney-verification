Bio

As a Registered Patent and Trademark Attorney and IP Coach®, Ken is experienced in helping companies build unique value and reach their goals.  Representing clients throughout the World, Ken’s emphasis is on strategic value planning and client education - with teaching experience as faculty and adjunct faculty at Universities both in the United States and China.  Ken is a firm believer that every company has value, and that proper use of intellectual property assets gives companies the boost they need to realize their maximum potential value.  With several successful company exits himself, invention recognition on over 60 patents, and extensive research into what creates company value, Ken is a valuable counselor for any company with a desire to create, take advantage of, and protect business opportunities.
